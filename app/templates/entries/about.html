Why did I decide to make this project?

**This project was to challenge people to use only 6 words to describe their days.

My name is Markel Gladney and my inspiration for creating this Django project comes from the fact that many people, including myself, struggle with expressing their thoughts concisely. To obtain this, I designed a diary that challenges users to sum up their day in just six words, encouraging reflection and brevity.
